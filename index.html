Ryan Fayyazi

I'm in my fourth year of an honours B.Sc. at the Univeristy of British Columbia, studying machine learning and theoretical neuroscience. Current research: * With Dr. Jeremy Seamans, I am characterizing the neural activity in the medial PFC and basolateral amygdala of rats during a multiarmed-bandit-style decision task, with non-stationary and uncertain reward probabilities. * With Dr. Frank Wood.

, I am modeling neural processing in C. elegans through inference of hidden neuron voltages from observed body positions. For my honours thesis, I am developing a pipeline for synthesizing non-linear learning rules from pre- and postsynaptic calcium observations during habituation. I want to understand the information processing underlying human planning, decision-making, and intuition.
