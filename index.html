Ryan Fayyazi

I'm in my fourth year of an honours B.Sc. at the Univeristy of British Columbia, studying computer science and neuroscience.

Current research:

  • Jeremy Seamans Lab: Characterizing activity in rat mPFC and BLA during multiarmed-bandit-style decision-making with non-stationary and uncertain reward probabilities.
  • Frank Wood Lab: Using an ODE-based simulation of the C. elegans nervous system, I'm (1) infering hidden neural voltages from observed body-positions, and (2) automatically synthesizing synaptic learning rules underlying habituation.

    • My long-term goal is to model the information processing underlying human planning, decision-making, and intuition.
